\begin{ThesisAbstracten}
This bachelor thesis deals with migration of web archive created by the project WebArchiv. At the beginning the thesis introduces the project WebArchiv and continues with an overview of tools and listing of related studies.

After review and summary of information it continues with archive formats survey. The advantages and disadvantages of each format are presented. It is shown why it is necessary to migrate the existing archive.

The last part deals with a real solution to archive migration. The purpose is to present the advantages and disadvantages of different instruments and modify and implement the most useful of them for the purposes of the WebArchiv project. The thesis is then a groundwork for the migration of the whole repository of data.
